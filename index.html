Features

Stunning Display

The Dell XPS 15 has a stunning display. Immerse yourself in content with bright, colorful panels with high resolution and more display area to keep you productive. The XPS 15 also features accurate resolution and 100% Adobe RGB color gamut that delivers vivid, bright, and accurate colors.

Optional OLED and 4K+ display available

Powerful Performance

The Dell XPS 15 have a perfect balance of size and performance that fuels your productivity. The Dell XPS 15 is equipped with the latest Intel® Core™ processors. This makes browsing, editing, and other computing tasks a breeze.

You can also avail the optional NVIDIA® GeForce RTX™ 3050 Ti GPUs which also provide a performance boost for your graphics-intensive workflows.

Precision Craftsmanship

The Dell XPS 15 is crafted from authentic, high-quality materials and precision-cut for a flawless finish in a durable, lightweight design.

The XPS 15 also features a comfortable touchpad and an edge-to-edge backlit keyboard with larger keycaps for maximum productivity.

Incredible Sound

The XPS 15 is the first-ever laptop to feature Waves Nx 3D audio for speakers. Tune in to an immersive sound experience like you've never experienced before.

Fast and secure log-on

The new XPS 15 offers a multitude of innovations that enable greater productivity more quickly. Log-on instantly using the laptop's built-in lid sensor, which turns it on in milliseconds. You can also log-on safely with Windows Hello or fingerprint reader.

Long Battery Life

With the Dell XPS 15, you'll experience incredibly long battery life. You can stream up to 13 hours on an FHD+ model with 86WHr battery and up to 10 hours on an OLED model with 86WHr battery.

Powered by Windows 11

Enjoy the new features of the latest Windows 11 operating system on the Dell XPS 15.
